# DRAKHART — Story & Lore

## The World: "The Shattered Continent"

Once, dragons ruled the skies. Their hearts were furnaces of raw creation — each beat a pulse of elemental power. Humanity, in its hubris, learned to harvest these hearts and forge them into **Guymelefs**: biomechanical war machines of bone, metal, and dragon-fire.

The **Dragon War** consumed the continent. Empires rose and fell in a single generation. When the war ended, the world was ash. The dragons were gone. Only their hearts remained — scattered across the ruins, waiting.

The **Iron Empire** rose from the ashes, not to rebuild, but to conquer what remained. They seek every last Dragon Core to forge an unstoppable army of Guymelefs. The continent's few survivors call this the **Final Forging**.

You are one of them.

## The Protagonist: The Warden

A nameless warrior of the old guard. You wear the green armor of the **Wardens** — an order sworn to protect the Dragon Cores from those who would corrupt them. Most Wardens are dead. You should be too.

But you found a Core that still beats.

When you touched it, it didn't burn you. It *recognized* you.

Now the Core lives inside your chest. It whispers in a language older than steel. It shows you visions — battles you never fought, skies you never flew. It wants to become something more. It wants to become you.

**Personality**: Silent, determined, haunted. The Warden doesn't speak because there's no one left to speak to. The few NPCs you meet — echoes of the dead, fragments of broken machines — do the talking.

**Design philosophy**: The Warden is a vessel for the player. Minimal backstory, maximum presence. You define the Warden through action.

## The Antagonist: The Iron Empire

Led by the **Iron Chancellor**, a figure never seen but felt everywhere. The Empire's philosophy is simple: dragons were weapons. We will use them better than our ancestors did. Every Guymelef they capture, they corrupt. Every Core they find, they forge into a soldier.

The Empire's forces:

- **Iron Guard** — Humanoid mecha with heavy armor. Slow but devastating. (Mecha zone enemies)
- **Sky Hunters** — Aerial drones that patrol the skies. (Dragon/shmup zone enemies)
- **Corrupted Guymelefs** — Captured dragon-mecha, twisted by imperial technology. (Bosses)
- **The Forgemaster** — The Empire's chief engineer. Never seen, but his work is everywhere: barricades, traps, corrupted cores.

## The Tarot: Echoes of Destiny

Before the Dragon War, seers read fate in cards. The 10 Major Arcana that survived the war are called **War Echoes** — each one imprinted with the dying will of a dragon or the final thought of a fallen Warden.

When you find a War Echo, you absorb its power. The memory burns into you. You see how they died. You gain what they lost.

The cards are not magic. They are *inheritance*.

## Key Story Beats

### Act I: Awakening
- Warden wakes in the Ashen Forest, alone
- Fights through the forest, finds the first Dragon Core
- **Core speaks**: *"You are not the first. You will not be the last. But you are now."*
- Unlocks MECHA form
- **Boss foreshadowing**: distant roar of a Corrupted Guymelef

### Act II: Iron and Fire
- Enter the Iron Bastion — Empire forward outpost
- Smash barricades, fight Iron Guard
- Find the Sky Core — a dragon heart suspended in an ancient forge
- **Core speaks**: *"The sky remembers. Do you?"*
- Unlocks DRAGON form
- First flight sequence

### Act III: Storm
- Storm Gorge — the dragon's graveyard
- Forced-scroll shmup through the gorge
- **Boss 1**: Corrupted Guymelef — an ancient dragon-mecha twisted by the Empire
- Drops **Strength** tarot card: *"She did not fall. She was pushed."*

### Act IV: Depth
- Crystal Mines — where the Empire digs for more Cores
- Underground maze, backtracking opportunities
- **Boss 2**: Crystal Guardian — a golem animated by shattered Core fragments
- Drops **The Tower** tarot card: *"He held the line. Alone. For three days."*

### Act V: Ascent
- Sky Temple — a floating monastery where Wardens once trained
- Vertical climb, wind mechanics
- **Boss 3**: Sky Empress — a corrupted Guymelef of imperial royalty
- Drops **The Emperor** tarot card: *"She chose the crown over the heart."*

### Act VI: The Core
- The heart of the Iron Empire's forge
- Alternating gameplay: platforming → mecha → shmup
- **Final Boss**: The Iron Heart — a colossal Guymelef powered by dozens of corrupted Cores
- Three phases, one per form
- **Ending**: The Iron Heart collapses. The Cores are freed. They scatter across the continent, seeding new life. The Warden walks into the ash, the last Core still beating in their chest.

## Post-Game / Secrets

- **True ending**: Collect all 10 War Echoes before the final boss. The freed Cores speak in unison, revealing the Warden's true name.
- **Secret boss**: The Forgemaster — hidden in a chamber beneath The Core. Requires all three forms at maximum power. Drops a unique weapon skin.
- **Boss rush mode**: Unlocked after completion. Fight all bosses in sequence with limited energy.
